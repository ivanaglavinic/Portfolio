Classic Pancakes
Ingredients:

    1 cup flour
    2 tbsp sugar
    1 tbsp baking powder
    1/2 tsp salt
    1 egg
    1 cup milk
    2 tbsp melted butter
    1 tsp vanilla (optional)

Instructions:

    Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt).
    Whisk wet ingredients (egg, milk, butter, vanilla).
    Combine wet and dry ingredients, stir gently.
    Heat a skillet, grease lightly, and pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
    Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook 1-2 minutes more.
    Serve with toppings of choice.
